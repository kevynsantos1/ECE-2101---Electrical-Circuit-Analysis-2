In this lab, we will be analyzing the voltage relationships in a series resonant circuit. More
specifically, how we get our resonant frequency and quality factor from our resistor, inductor,
and capacitor values. It is important to understand that at resonant frequency our circuit will be
purely resistive and our current will be at its maximum value. Below resonant frequency our
circuit is capacitive, and above resonant frequency, it will be inductive. To move forward in this
experiment our quality factor, Q, equations will be very important.
