In this lab, we will examine the voltage relationships in series R, L, and C circuits. First, we
build two circuits containing RC and RL components in series. We will measure and calculate
the magnitudes and phases of the voltages of the components in both circuits and determine their
impedances. We note that for resistors, the voltage is in phase with the current, for capacitors the
voltage lags the current by 90 degrees, and for inductors, the voltage leads the current by 90
degrees. Capacitors and inductors unlike resistors are also frequency-dependent thus, we will see
what happens when our source frequency changes as well.
