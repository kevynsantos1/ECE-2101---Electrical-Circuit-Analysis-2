In this lab, we will analyze the behaviors of capacitors and inductors in different scenarios. 
We will charge and discharge capacitors and calculate the initial voltages across each of the 
capacitors to see if the theoretical values match our experimental values. We will also do 
similar calculation and experimentation for inductors in order to confirm their behavior 
theoretically and experimentally. In order to calculate the theoretical values of these circuits, 
we will use thevenien equivalent circuits, and steady state assumptions such as a capacitor 
becoming an open circuit and the inductor turning into a short.
